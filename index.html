A good contract helps you

  • Get paid on time, with clear terms your client agreed to in writing before the work begins
  • Protect yourself when a client shows up late, cancels last minute, or changes the scope mid-project
  • Keep ownership of your creative work unless you explicitly choose to transfer it
  • Set boundaries around revisions, reshoots, and additional requests that were not in the original agreement
  • Have something real to stand behind if a client tries to dispute payment or use your work without permission
